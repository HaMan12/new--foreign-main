Epidemic Prevention Dissemination
  • Wearing a face mask is mandatory in following places, also please maintain social distancing, Indoor 1.5 meters, outdoor 1 meter of social safety distance, keep hands clean.

    Medical Institution, Public Transportation, Markets, Education and Learning Places, Exhibition Places, Religious Places, Entertainment Places, Public Institutions

  • Penal provision

    Those who fail to comply and follow the regulations will be fined by the local government from NT$3,000 to NT$15,000.

  • If you have symptoms such as fever, cough, abnormal sense of smell, diarrhea, please wear a mask and seek medical treatment immediately, taking public transportation is prohibited and inform the doctor of your travel history, occupation, contact history and whether in The pandemic prevention for large-scale gathering.

  • Open
